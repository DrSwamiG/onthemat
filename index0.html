Course Overview

The mini course is structured around four weekly movements. Week 1 introduces the seeker, the cowrie snail, and sacred question-asking. Week 2 explores the five families of responses. Week 3 builds the art of casting and documentation. Week 4 applies Four Cowrie to real-world decisions and ethics.
