Days 22–30

Application & Integration

Focus: real-world application, divination ethics, shadow work, and evaluating readiness for deeper Four Cowrie or other Orisha-based training.
Day 22

Divining for Yourself

Today you ask Four Cowrie directly what you need to focus on in this present week. Allow the answer to guide your schedule, not just your thoughts.

Assignment

  • Ask: “Is this the primary focus I need for the coming week?” and cast.
  • Translate the answer into one concrete action or boundary.

Reflection Prompt

How honest was I in stating the question, and where did I hold back?

Self reading Focus
Day 23

Divining for Others — Ethics

Before reading for others, clarify your standards. Four Cowrie is powerful in the hands of a person committed to truth, consent, and protection of the seeker’s dignity.

Assignment

  • Write seven personal rules for ethical divination (e.g., confidentiality, consent, no promises).
  • If appropriate, perform one simple reading for a willing person using only one question.

Reflection Prompt

Where might ego, fear, or the desire to impress interfere with my clarity?

Ethics Service
Day 24

Troubleshooting “Bad” Casts

Sometimes answers feel contradictory or disappointing. Instead of discarding them, treat them as feedback on question design, timing, or your emotional readiness.

Assignment

  • Choose three castings that felt wrong, confusing, or disappointing.
  • For each, ask: “What might this answer have been trying to protect or reveal?”

Reflection Prompt

What story emerges when I place these three “bad” castings side by side?

Troubleshooting Meta-view
Day 25

Power of Word — Okanran Revisited

Okanran is associated with the power of speech and the risk of negative inner dialogue. Today you listen for how your words shape the questions you bring to the shells.

Assignment

  • Ask: “Is my self-talk supporting my destiny in this season?” and cast.
  • Write three replacement phrases for any harmful self-talk you notice.

Reflection Prompt

Which phrase of self-talk most needs to be retired right now?

Okanran Speech
Day 26

Ejife — Three-Position Spread

Today you use multiple casts together. While Four Cowrie often works with single questions, a short spread can reveal movement through time.

Assignment

  • Cast three times with these questions: “What is leaving?”, “What is arriving?”, “What is hidden?”
  • Summarize the three results as one short story.

Reflection Prompt

What transition is the story pointing toward?

3-cast spread Story
Day 27

Alafia Day — Blessing Work

Center your attention on the energies of ease, consent, and blessing. Allow yourself to recognize where things are already flowing well.

Assignment

  • Ask a blessing-oriented question, such as “Is this project blessed for me to continue?”
  • Create a gratitude ritual or offering based on the answer.

Reflection Prompt

What blessing have I been refusing to see because I was focused on struggle?

Alafia Gratitude
Day 28

Oyeku Day — Shadow & Protection

Today you consciously engage topics that evoke fear or resistance, approaching them with the understanding that a firm “No” can be a shield as much as a barrier.

Assignment

  • Ask one question you have been afraid to bring to the shells.
  • If Oyeku appears, pause and ask what is being protected.

Reflection Prompt

Which area of life do I most avoid exploring with honest divination?

Shadow Protection
Day 29

Thirty-Cast Cycle

This intensive cycle helps you see which response family has been governing your recent path. It also mirrors how often you are willing to ask for guidance.

Assignment

  • Complete 30 castings over the day or in focused blocks.
  • Calculate the percentage of each response family (Alafia, Etawa, Ejife, Okanran, Oyeku).

Reflection Prompt

Which family appears as your current “governing shell,” and how does that feel?

Data Governing family
Day 30

Readiness Evaluation & Next Path

The final day is a conversation between you, your higher self, and your Four Cowrie archive. You are not grading yourself but listening for whether you are ready for deeper work or need more time at this level.

Assignment

  • Prepare a simple packet for review: your 21-day casting log, the 30-cast summary from Day 29, and your top five insights.
  • Write one paragraph on what kind of Four Cowrie or Orisha-based training you feel called to next.

Reflection Prompt

Do I feel invited to more structured study, or to sit quietly with what I have received so far?

Readiness Integration
