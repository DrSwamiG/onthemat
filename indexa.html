Four Cowrie Shell — PreLesson 0.0

This is an offering for enlightenment seekers — those who discover that blessings can appear as calamity, disaster, and delay. This course provides tools to transform distraction into blessedness and nourish self-realization.

Motivation

Enlightenment is not what it appears to be, yet it is always beneficial. Self-realization is temporary, but the discipline that supports it is permanent. This course encourages clarity of purpose, deeper self-insight, and the reassembly of the fragmented self within an ancestral framework.

Cowrie Snail

Cowries are marine snails with a smooth shell and a ventral opening lined with ridges (“teeth”). In divination, this opening is treated as the “mouth.” We use cowrie shells (not live snails) as sacred instruments for communication and insight.

Asking Questions

Only ask questions that require a “yes” or “no” response. Avoid harmful, repetitive, already-known, or ill-formed questions. The clarity of your question determines the clarity of the shell’s response.

Orientation & Casting

• Skyward (open mouth) → success • Earthward (closed mouth) → success after struggle

When all four shells are cast, you may receive one of the five families of response:

• Alafia — Yes
• Ejife — Definitely
• Etawa — Probably
• Okanran — Probably Not
• Oyeku — No

Optional Assignment

Practice casting daily for 21 consecutive days. Complete the work within two lunar cycles. Document and name each cast. Submissions may be reviewed within two cycles.
